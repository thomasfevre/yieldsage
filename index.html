Yield Sustainability Flags

★
Auto-flag strategies based on yield source. >50% incentivized = "potentially non-durable". APY >15% = "compensating high risk". APY >30% = "extreme, typically leveraged".

Industry consensus: 3-8% APY = safe zone from real economic activity. Above = risk premium.

Implementation Order

Risk Score Framework (Industry Consensus)

Based on analysis of methodologies from vaults.fyi, Exponential.fi, Gauntlet, Chaos Labs, Credora, DeFi Safety, and the EEA. All converge on the same 6 dimensions.

Dimension Weight What We Measure Current Coverage
Time & Survival 20% Protocol age, peak TVL, survived stress events (UST, FTX), incident history Not covered
Dependency & Composability 15% Number of protocols involved, dependency graph, correlation between collaterals Partial (category)
Liquidity & Exit 15% Exit mechanism (instant/FIFO/lockup), secondary market, $1M exit slippage Partial (lockup_days)
Oracle Integrity 15% Oracle type (Pyth/Switchboard/custom), decentralization, fallback mechanism Not covered
Audit & OpSec 15% Audit count & quality, freshness, multisig setup, timelock, open source Mostly covered
Yield Source 20% Real yield vs incentives ratio, yield classification, sustainability zone Partial (base/incentive %)

Score: 0-100 per dimension, weighted sum for final score. This aligns with the call for "L2Beat but for DeFi risk" - a public, transparent standard.

Discussion & Open Questions

Topics for team brainstorming. What's missing? What should we prioritize?

For the Team

  • Scope: Should this be AllezLabs-internal only, or could the risk framework become a public product (the "L2Beat for Solana DeFi risk" that the industry is asking for)?
  • Budget: Tier 1 is $0, Tier 2 is $0-50/mo, Tier 3 is $50-200/mo. What's the budget we're comfortable with?
  • Deployment: Currently runs on a local machine. For team use, we need a server (cloud VM, or Modal for serverless?). What's the preferred infra?
  • Protocol priority: Which protocols should we deep-monitor first? (Kamino seems obvious for AllezLabs, but Drift post-exploit is the best-documented)
  • Alert destination: Dedicated Slack channel? Who should receive critical alerts? On-call rotation?
  • Integration with existing tools: Does this replace any current monitoring, or complement it? Any overlap with Chaos Labs/Gauntlet dashboards we already use?

Missing Features We Haven't Thought Of?

  • Governance monitoring: Track on-chain governance proposals that could change risk parameters
  • Insurance integration: Flag whether a strategy has cover available (Nexus Mutual, InsurAce)
  • Historical APY tracking: "This pool was at 20% APY last week, now 5% - is that normal or a red flag?"
  • Portfolio simulation: "If I put $100K in this allocation, what's my worst-case 30-day scenario?"
  • Competitive intelligence: Track what vaults competitors are integrating or launching
  • Regulatory watch: Better filtering for regulations that actually impact Solana DeFi specifically
  • Whale tracking: Monitor large wallets (known funds, teams) for position changes as signals
